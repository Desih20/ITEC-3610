Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive enhancement is a step-by-step process. When developing a website, you have to add the content first (HTML), then add the design (CSS), and last but not least the interactivity (Javascript), which is what progressive enhancement is. One of the benefits of progressive enhancment is "stability and foundation" (Jamalzadeh, 2023). Progressive enhancement helps web developers focus on one thing at time and follow a structure when building websites. This prevents confusion, errors, and stress.
